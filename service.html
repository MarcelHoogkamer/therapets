Hire a pet for a therapy
A therapy animal is any companion animal who has undergone training and certification
to provide comfort and affectionate support to someone other than their owner.

Adopt a pet
You’ll change a life. You’ll get a healthy, vet-checked pet. You’ll save money.
You won’t be supporting puppy mills or pet stores. The pet will be behavior assessed. Depending on their background,
the pet may already be trained and know basic commands. You’ll receive ongoing assistance as needed from the shelter.
You'll feel better.

What if? I want to do home therapy but can't have a pet at home?

Not everyone has the opportunity to have a pet at home, even though they would like to undergo pet therapy. For these cases, we offer you the option of a space where you can spend time during the therapy.
